Switzerland

Where the mountains touch the sky and every view feels like a painting.

Why I Want to Visit

Switzerland is my mom's dream destination because of its breathtaking mountains, peaceful lakes, and beautiful scenery. I want to join her and experience the natural beauty of the Swiss Alps and the clean, relaxing environment. It feels like a perfect place to enjoy nature and find peace.

Places I Want to See

I hope to visit the Swiss Alps, Lake Geneva, Interlaken, and the charming city of Zurich. I also want to ride a scenic train through the mountains and see snow-covered landscapes.

My Dream Experience

One day, I want to experience walking in a small Swiss village, tasting authentic Swiss chocolate, and enjoying the fresh mountain air. Visiting Switzerland would truly be a peaceful and unforgettable experience for me.
