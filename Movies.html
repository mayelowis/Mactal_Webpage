My Film Movies

These are some of my favorite movies that I enjoy watching. They have a special place in my heart and I love sharing them with others.

Romance

Beautiful love stories that touch the heart.

Titanic

Director: James Cameron

Stars: Leonardo DiCaprio, Kate Winslet

A sweeping romantic adventure set against the real‑life sinking of the RMS Titanic in 1912. Young artist Jack Dawson and aristocrat Rose DeWitt Bukater fall in love aboard the ill‑fated ship, but their romance is tested when the ocean liner collides with an iceberg and begins to sink.

Musical

Uplifting and inspiring musicals full of songs and performances.

The Greatest Showman

Director: Michael Gracey

Stars: Hugh Jackamn, Zac Efron, Michelle Williams, Rebecca Ferguson, Zendaya

A musical drama inspired by the life of P.T. Barnum, an ambitious showman who rises from humble beginnings to create the iconic Barnum & Bailey Circus, celebrating spectacle and uniqueness while facing both success and personal challenges.

Adventure / Family

Heartwarming stories of courage and loyalty.

Togo

Director: Ericson Core

Stars: Willem Dafoe, Julianne Nicholson, Christopher Heyerdahl, Michael Greyeyes, Nikolai Nokolaeff

Based on the true story of sled dog Togo and musher Leonhard Seppala, this adventure follows their incredible journey across harsh Alaskan terrain during the 1925 serum run to Nome to deliver life‑saving diphtheria antitoxin to a town in crisis.
