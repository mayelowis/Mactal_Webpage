My Film Movies

These are some of my favorite movies that I enjoy watching.

Romantic

Beautiful love stories that touch the heart.

Titanic

Director: James Cameron

Stars: Leonardo DiCaprio, Kate Winslet

Rating: ★★★★★


Titanic feels like a whirlwind of romance and tragedy. Jack and Rose come from different worlds but fall deeply in love aboard the ship. Their story is passionate and unforgettable, but the sinking of the Titanic turns it into heartbreak scene. It makes me think about how love can be powerful even if it doesn’t last forever and love has the power to change lives.

Trailer from this Movie

All the Bright Places

Director: Brett Haley

Stars: Elle Fanning, Justice Smith

Rating: ★★★★★


This film feels heavy but deeply touching. Violet and Finch meet when they’re both struggling with pain and sadness. Together, they discover small joys and the beauty in life, but their journey also shows how fragile people can be. It’s a reminder that even in darkness, there can be light, and that love can change the way we see the world.

Trailer from this Movie

Musical

Uplifting musicals full of songs and performances.

The Greatest Showman

Director: Michael Gracey

Stars: Hugh Jackman, Zac Efron, Michelle Williams

Rating: ★★★★☆


The Greatest Showman film feels like a celebration of dreams and differences. P.T. Barnum builds a circus filled with people who are unique and often judged by society. The music is uplifting, the performances are dazzling, and the story reminds me that chasing dreams is worth it, even if the world doubts you. It’s about turning struggles into something beautiful. Also, i love the songs here in this movie

Trailer from this Movie

Encanto

Director: Byron Howard, Jared Bush

Stars: Stephanie Beatriz, John Leguizamo

Rating: ★★★★★


Encanto feels magical yet very real. The Madrigal family all have gifts except Mirabel, who feels left out. When their magic begins to fade, she discovers her true strength. It’s a story about family expectations, self-worth, and how sometimes the person who seems ordinary is the one who saves everyone. It makes me think about how love and acceptance are more powerful than any magic.

Trailer from this Movie

Drama

Powerful stories that explore human emotions and relationships.

Barcelona: A Love Untold

Director: Olivia M. Lamasan

Stars: Kathryn Bernardo, Daniel Padilla

Rating: ★★★★☆


This story feels bittersweet. Two young people meet in Barcelona while chasing their own dreams, but the both are carry heavy pasts. Their love grows slowly, with moments of joy and pain, reminding me that healing takes time and love isn’t always simple it should be take serious. It’s about second chances, letting go, and finding hope in unexpected places.

Trailer from this Movie

Forrest Gump

Director: Robert Zemeckis

Stars: Tom Hanks, Robin Wright, Gary Sinise

Rating: ★★★★★


Forrest’s journey feels like a gentle reminder that life doesn’t need to be complicated to be meaningful. He experiences huge moments in history, but what matters most to him is kindness and love, especially for Jenny. His simple way of living makes me reflect on how extraordinary life can be when you stay true to your heart.

Trailer from this Movie

Comedy

Movies that will make you laugh out loud.

Four Sisters and a Wedding

Director: Cathy Garcia-Molina

Stars: Bea Alonzo, Angel Locsin, Shaina Magdayao, Toni Gonzaga

Rating: ★★★★☆


The film feels like being inside a real family gathering. The sisters argue, tease, and meddle in their brother’s wedding plans, but beneath all the drama is deep love. It’s not just about stopping a wedding it’s about facing old wounds, forgiving each other, and realizing that family, no matter how messy, is worth fighting for.

Trailer from this Movie

The Hangover

Director: Todd Phillips

Stars: Bradley Cooper, Ed Helms, Zach Galifianakis

Rating: ★★★★☆


This film feels like being dropped into total madness. A group of friends wake up after a wild night in Las Vegas with no memory of what happened, and the groom is missing. As they retrace their steps, every discovery is crazier than the last. It’s hilarious but also shows how friendship can survive even the most ridiculous situations.
