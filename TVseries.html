MY TV SERIES

My favorite TV series that have captivated me with their compelling storytelling, memorable characters, and unforgettable moments.

Action and Drama

My favorite action series full of suspense, adventure, and thrilling moments.

Stranger Things

Director: Matt and Ross Duffer

Stars: Winona Ryder, David Harbour, Millie Bobby Brown

Rating: ★★★★★


A thrilling ride into the 1980s, Stranger Things instantly hooked me with its mix of nostalgia, suspense, and heart. It’s about a group of kids in Hawkins, Indiana, whose ordinary lives spiral into chaos when a mysterious girl with telekinetic powers appears and their friend goes missing. I loved the way it blends supernatural horror with genuine friendship, family bonds, and a touch of retro charm each season feels like a pulse pounding adventure I can’t put down.

Encantadia (2016)

Director: Marc A. Reyes

Stars: Glaiza de Castro, Kylie Padilla, Gabbi Garcia, Sanya Lopez

Rating: ★★★★★


This reboot of a Filipino fantasy epic is mesmerizing. Encantadia transported me to a world of magical kingdoms, fierce warriors, and elemental powers. I found myself completely invested in the sisters’ struggles to protect their realm and the intricate politics of the four kingdoms. The combination of breathtaking visuals, strong female leads, and epic storytelling made it feel when i was a kid like a modern fantasy classic I couldn’t stop watching every night.

Romantic

Beautiful love stories that touch the heart during the pandemic.

Crash landing in you

Director: Lee Jung-hyo

Stars: Hyun Bin, Son Ye-jin

Rating: ★★★★★


This drama felt like pure emotional magic during lock-down season. A South Korean heiress accidentally paraglides into North Korea and meets a stoic North Korean officer who becomes her unlikely protector. I was captivated by the tension, humor, and romance, the cultural contrasts added so much depth. Every episode had me laughing, crying, and desperately hoping they’d find a way to be together. It’s one of those shows that leaves you thinking about the characters long after it ends.

Trailer from this TV Series

Bridgerton

Director: Chris Van Dusen

Stars: Daphne Bridgerton, Simon Basset, Penelope Bridgerton

Rating: ★★★★★


Bridgerton is like stepping into a gorgeous, scandalous fairy tale. I was drawn to the glittering balls, forbidden romances, and witty gossip narrated by the mysterious Lady Whistledown. Each character has a depth that surprises you beyond the elegant costumes and fancy parties, and the way love, family expectations, and societal rules collide kept me completely hooked. It’s romantic, playful, and occasionally scandalous I binged it faster than I intended.
